## S1.07 Making Agendas {#s1.07-making-agendas}

**Owner:** Directors · **Last reviewed:** 2025 board (imported from the Hitchhiker's Guide, July 2026) · **Status:** current — when you find something stale, fix it and update this line.

Making an agenda involves:

1. Looking at the Master Splash Timeline and copying over any relevant tasks  
2. Adding any tasks leftover from last week  
3. Determining which tasks can be completed by directors, and which tasks can be completed by the admin team; make clear sections for each (put directors tasks on a separate page below everything else)  
4. For certain tasks, it may be convenient to pre-assign an admin (ie. tasks relevant to a specific “Czar” (see section S1.05))  
5. Determining any relevant team announcements, and place them at the top of the agenda (ie. teacher reg opens today)  
6. Add any updates, for example the number of teachers you have and the number you still need  
7. Adding any tasks relevant to the whole team  
8. Determine if any Take-Home tasks will be necessary, and add them to the agenda  
9. Determine if there are enough tasks so that all admins will have something to do during the meeting; if this is not the case, go back to the Master Timeline and start pulling events from future weeks that can be done early. It is always better to have too many tasks rather than too few  
10. Feel free to do something similar to “Admin of the Week” or come up with your own ideas for fun icebreakers/agenda items, provided that they take up less than a few minutes of meeting time; these shouldn’t be full on games or anything that will be too distracting.
