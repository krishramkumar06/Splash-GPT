## S1.05 Delegating Tasks {#s1.05-delegating-tasks}

**Owner:** Directors · **Last reviewed:** 2025 board (imported from the Hitchhiker's Guide, July 2026) · **Status:** current — when you find something stale, fix it and update this line.

Trying to assign people tasks during the middle of the semester when people are busy is much harder than assigning them responsibilities at the start of the semester--so definitely assign people to the following tasks at the start of the semester.

You can both decide which positions seem necessary to assign to others and which ones you will do on your own, but these are what I’d recommend. For Spring ‘19, we didn’t formally assign any of these positions, which led to us doing most of the work… so ultimately assigning these tasks will reduce your workload.

1. Teacher Recruitment Czar   
   For Fall ‘19, this will be Ananya; this task involves probably the most work because teacher recruitment is always a challenge. Directors are ultimately responsible for ensuring that teacher recruitment happens, and for working directly with this person to coordinate what needs to get done. Some of this job will likely end up being shared by the directors just because it can be so time consuming/difficult.

2. Class Approvals, Teacher forms, and Materials Request Czar  
   This task should be shared by the directors but parts of it can be doled out to admins to reduce the workload; ultimately though, it is important that both directors have an idea of all the classes being taught, for two reasons (1) this makes it easiest to resolve problems on the day-of (2) directors **need** to be aware of special room requirements for scheduling purposes. This role consists of approving classes and noting down materials requests/other notable things (ie. if a class needs the computer lab, a kitchen, or is meeting in a classroom and then leaving for some other location). It also consists of keeping track of teacher forms and approving/not approving materials requests. All of the work done by admins for this task should be checked by the directors.  
     
3. School Groups Czar  
   This person will have little to do until student registration opens; this task is also a significant responsibility though, later on. Basically this person has to email all the school groups continually and check their forms over, etc. This is another job where director oversight is key.  
     
4. Financial Aid & Payment Approval Czar  
   This task is probably the least time consuming, and likely amounts to 4 hours of work or so over a two week period. This task is also easily learned, but is important. This task consists of updating the financial aid and sending payment reminders every 3 days over a two week period.  
 


5. Parent Program Czar  
   This person is in charge of planning the whole parent program. This actually doesn’t take that much time, and **should be done as soon as possible, meaning in the first 1-3 meetings.**
