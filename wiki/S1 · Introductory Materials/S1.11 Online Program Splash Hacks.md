### Moderators

Moderators are required by Yale Risk Management so that there are two “adults” in the zoom rooms with students at all times. Search these items in the Google Drive for more information.

* Moderator Cheat Sheet: minute-by-minute instructions for moderators  
* Moderator Training Slides

As of Spring 2021, you can now schedule moderators on the website.

* All moderators should create a teacher account and fill out their availability on the teacher registration page. When prompted to moderate, moderators should check “will moderate” and fill out the subsequent fields.  
* After teacher registration closes, site admins can edit any user’s availability: search user \> view: current program \> check/edit availability  
* If you need to manually fill out the moderator form for someone: admin pages \> program \> moderator records \> add moderator record (or use [this link](https://yale.learningu.org/admin/program/moderatorrecord/add/))  
* When scheduling moderators, uncheck “hide sections that don’t match preferred categories” for easier scheduling
