## S1.06 Weekly Directors’ Meetings {#s1.06-weekly-directors’-meetings}

**Owner:** Directors · **Last reviewed:** 2025 board (imported from the Hitchhiker's Guide, July 2026) · **Status:** current — when you find something stale, fix it and update this line.

Weekly directors meetings consist of:

1. Making the agenda (see S1.07 below)  
2. Resolving pressing emails that require the input of both directors  
3. Getting a jump on any director’s tasks; directors tasks should be things that are either (1) very important to be done in a timely/proper manner (2) would take just as long to explain to someone else as it would to do yourself (for example, in the vast majority of cases you shouldn’t be asking admins to respond to emails for you…)  
4. Assigning director’s tasks to one another
