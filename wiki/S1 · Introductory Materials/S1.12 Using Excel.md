## S1.12 Using Excel {#s1.12-using-excel}

**Owner:** Directors · **Last reviewed:** 2025 board (imported from the Hitchhiker's Guide, July 2026) · **Status:** current — when you find something stale, fix it and update this line.

Knowledge of Excel Macros, Pivot Tables on Excel/Google Sheets, and knowing basic excel functions will save you a lot of time. Things like organizing data from student feedback surveys or sending mass emails via the gmail are two examples, but generally, knowing how to use Excel well will be very helpful.
