## S6.02 Transition checklist — directors

**Owner:** Outgoing directors · **Last reviewed:** July 2026 · **Status:** current — when you find something stale, fix it and update this line.

Directors hand off to directors. The handoff is **done when every box is checked**, not when the semester ends. Outgoing and incoming directors walk this list together in one sitting (~90 minutes); the incoming pair keeps the checked copy in their program folder.

### Before the meeting (outgoing directors)

- [ ] Final Phil for your program is complete, including the wiki-edits table (S0.03)
- [ ] All assigned wiki edits from your Phil are actually made, "Last reviewed" lines updated
- [ ] Your program folder in `1 · Current Semester` is complete (emails sent, money, teacher docs filed) and has been moved to `4 · Program Archive`
- [ ] Splash HQ Setup config for your semester is saved into the Handbook folder (`Download config file`)

### At the handoff meeting

- [ ] Gmail: incoming directors can log in; walk through the labeling/archiving system (S1.04)
- [ ] Website: incoming directors have admin accounts on yale.learningu.org with correct permissions (S1.10)
- [ ] Splash HQ: show all four tabs; incoming directors open the app and load the config
- [ ] Walk through S1.01 (responsibilities) and your Phil's "advice to next year" section out loud
- [ ] Tour the Drive top level: `1 · Current Semester`, `2 · Handbook`, `3 · Org Essentials`, `4 · Program Archive` — and the three rules in `_START HERE`
- [ ] Introduce incoming directors to their mentor and to the presidents
- [ ] Hand over anything physical: keys, storage access, leftover supplies inventory, the printer situation

### After the meeting (incoming directors)

- [ ] Create your program folders in `1 · Current Semester` from the template (six subfolders + a blank Phil doc)
- [ ] Read S0.01, S0.02, S1.01, S1.02 within the first week
- [ ] Read your predecessors' Phil and the one before it — the mistakes repeat when this step is skipped
- [ ] Set the semester's dates in Splash HQ Setup and export the calendar
