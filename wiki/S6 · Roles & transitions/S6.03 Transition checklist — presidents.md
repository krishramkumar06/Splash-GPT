## S6.03 Transition checklist — presidents

**Owner:** Outgoing presidents · **Last reviewed:** July 2026 · **Status:** current — when you find something stale, fix it and update this line.

Presidents hand off to presidents, and the bar is higher than the directors' handoff because so much presidential knowledge has historically never been written down. **The core deliverable of a presidential transition is the Presidents Guide (S6.01): you may not hand off while it still contains a ⬜ stub you had the knowledge to fill.**

### Before the meeting (outgoing presidents)

- [ ] Presidents Guide (S6.01) audited top to bottom: every section you know how to do is written out; anything you never learned is flagged honestly as unknown
- [ ] Year-end SuperPHIL is complete and filed in the year's `Semester-wide` archive folder
- [ ] Finances closed out: semester actuals spreadsheet finished and filed in `3 · Org Essentials/Finances/`, all reimbursements paid, outstanding invoices listed
- [ ] 990/tax status written down: what was filed, when, what's due next and when
- [ ] `3 · Org Essentials/Accounts & access/` is complete and current: Gmail backup codes regenerated, Instagram info correct, no account exists that isn't listed there

### At the handoff meeting

- [ ] Log into every account together: Gmail (transfer 2FA/recovery to incoming presidents' devices), Instagram, tax/990 account, anything else in Accounts & access
- [ ] Walk through the Presidents Guide section by section — incoming presidents interrupt with questions; anything you have to explain verbally that isn't in the guide gets added to the guide *that day*
- [ ] Money walkthrough: bank/funding source access, who can spend, how reimbursements actually get paid
- [ ] Yale bureaucracy walkthrough: major event registration status, room reservation contacts, stakeholders meeting expectations
- [ ] LU walkthrough: current contacts, website admin access at the org level
- [ ] Board walkthrough: who's on it, how officers transition, open questions from the last board meeting
- [ ] Introduce incoming presidents to the mentors, the directors, and (by email) the LU and Yale contacts

### After the meeting (incoming presidents)

- [ ] Read S0.02 (Values & the permanent record) — enforcing it is now your job
- [ ] Read the last two SuperPHILs
- [ ] Calendar the recurring obligations for the year: 990 deadline, major event registration, stakeholders meeting, Splatreat, SuperPHIL
- [ ] Confirm the directors' transitions (S6.02) actually happened for both program pairs
