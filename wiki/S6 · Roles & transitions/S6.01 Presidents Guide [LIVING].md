## S6.01 Presidents Guide [LIVING]

**Owner:** Presidents · **Last reviewed:** July 2026 (skeleton — sections marked ⬜ need the current presidents to fill them in) · **Status:** incomplete by design — every president is required to leave this guide better than they found it.

The directors have a 300-page Hitchhiker's Guide; the presidents have historically had almost nothing written down, which makes the presidency the single most fragile knowledge role in the org. This guide fixes that. **Sections marked ⬜ are stubs**: they list what we know exists (with Drive locations), but the "how" lives in the current presidents' heads until they write it down here.

### What presidents do (vs. directors)

Directors run *programs*; presidents run *the organization*: finances, the board, Yale bureaucracy, Learning Unlimited (LU) relations, admin recruitment/retention, and continuity between semesters. The board-member role doc is here: `3 · Org Essentials/Evergreen how-tos/boardmemberrole` (Drive shortcut). ⬜ *Expand: how the board is structured, how often it meets, how officers are chosen.*

### The one rule of this guide

Every time you do something presidential that isn't in this guide, you owe the guide a paragraph. Your successors can't inherit what you didn't write.
