### S5.04 Ordering Food {#s5.04-ordering-food}

**Owner:** Directors · **Last reviewed:** 2025 board (imported from the Hitchhiker's Guide, July 2026) · **Status:** current — when you find something stale, fix it and update this line.

* Pizza \- Sprout lunch weeks 1&3, Splash lunch:  
  * Cheese, veggie, pepperoni; XL pizzas  
  * Papa John’s: 203-776-7272  
  * Call Wednesday/Thursday before program for Sprout, Monday/Tuesday before for Splash  
    * **Make sure to use Yale50 discount**  
    * **Tell them Splash at Yale & they tax-exempt it**  
    * For Splash: deliver to WLH (100 wall street)  
  * Estimating numbers:  
    * An XL pizza has 10 slices; each kid gets 2 slices → 1 pizza feeds 5 kids  
    * Take the number of kids who have requested each type from the website, divide by 5, and round up to the nearest whole pizza  
    * **Don’t add extra for teachers and admins;** if you order based off of the numbers on the website, with yield you’ll have enough extra for teachers/admins to eat  
  * Provides delivery \- set delivery time to 30 minutes before lunch so have time to set up  
* Subs \- Sprout lunch week 2, Splash lunch:  
  * Chicken sub, ham sub, veggie sub; 6-inch subs  
  * Subway on Chapel St.: 203-497-9324, ask for manager  
  * Call Monday/Tuesday before programs  
    * Mention order is from Splash at Yale and ask for previous price ($4/sandwich as of spring 2018\) \- $4.50 as of fall sprout  
  * Will want to get prepaid by check (they will want cash, but say we can’t do that)  
  * Estimating numbers:  
    * Use numbers of who requested what directly from website  
    * As with pizza, don’t add extra for teachers and admins  
  * Does not deliver; requires pickup \- set pickup time to 45 minutes before lunch so have time to set up  
* Plates/napkins/cups:  
  * Costco (Make a trip at the beginning of the semester to buy for both Sprout and Splash)
